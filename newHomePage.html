Our Team

We are a group of 5 college students who built this project in our design thinking lab. Our aim is to promote carpooling and make it more accessible to people, thereby reducing traffic congestion and carbon emissions. Each member of our team has unique skills and expertise, which we leveraged to build this website.

Our Aim

We aim to reduce traffic congestion, air pollution and parking problems by encouraging carpooling. Our platform connects drivers and riders to share rides and split the costs. By carpooling, we can make our commutes more sustainable and affordable.
